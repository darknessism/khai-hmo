Part 01

What is HMO and why
did Ping An re-architect it?

Health Maintenance Organization — a model that accounts for over 90% of the US commercial insurance market. But Ping An didn't just copy it — they completely re-architected it, making the "Integrated Finance + Health and Senior Care" strategy the group's core mission.

Ping An Finance Center · Shenzhen
"A world-leading integrated finance, health and senior care services group"
The official mission statement from Ping An's 2024 Annual Results — this is no longer just an insurance group

According to the official 2024 annual results report, Ping An defines itself as a "world-leading integrated finance, health and senior care services group" — no longer just an insurance company. The strategy focuses on two parallel pillars: Integrated Finance (insurance, banking, asset management) and Health and Senior Care (medical, elderly care, health technology). Technology enablement is the common foundation.

The starting point is completely different from UnitedHealth. Ping An began as a comprehensive retail financial company with an existing base of 242 million customers — healthcare was not its primary industry, it was a differentiating tool to retain and increase the value of its financial customers.

From the 2024 report: "Customers entitled to service benefits in the health & senior care ecosystem contribute about 70% of Ping An Life's NBV." — This is the official confirmation: the health ecosystem is not a cost — it is a primary source of NBV.

Part 05

AI is the Backbone:
"9+5+3" Proposition & "7+N+1" Product Matrix

Ping An calls its AI formula "9+5+3": 9 databases + 5 labs + 3 tech companies.

The nine databases process over 1 billion data entries per day on average, covering 240 million financial customers, providing deep insights into user needs and enabling user experience improvements.
1. Financial Services Data: Covers insurance, banking, and investment data (representing 240 million financial customers).
2. Healthcare Data: Data from Ping An's health ecosystem (e.g., Ping An Health).
3. Senior Care Data: Data related to senior care concierge services.
4. Auto Services Data: Data for AI image-based vehicle damage assessment and auto insurance.
5. Real Estate Services Data: Data from real estate-related business activities.
6. Smart City Services Data: Data supporting government and city management.
7. Customer Insight Data: Behavioral data enabling deep insights into user needs.
8. User Experience/Service Data: Data from AI-powered call centers and chatbots.
9. AI Training Data: Data used to train models in the five labs (e.g., Computer Vision, NLP).

The five labs (Micro-Expression Lab, Computer Vision Lab, Speech & NLP Lab, Data Analytics Lab, and Silicon Valley Lab) continuously explore cutting-edge technologies.

The three tech member companies (Ping An Technology, Ping An Health, and OneConnect) focus on expanding the breadth and depth of AI application scenarios. The Good Doctor 2025 IR also announced the "7+N+1" AI product matrix with 100+ business scenarios.

Part 07

5 Strategic Insights:
Drawn from Real Data

Things you can't see from just the headline numbers — but are confirmed by data from both official reports.

Ping An · "Expertise Makes Life Easier" — official 2024 tagline
Insight 01 · Confirmed by 2024 Annual Results
Healthcare as a "loss leader" — has been proven
The official numbers: customers using the health eco contribute 70% of Ping An Life's NBV. FYP/policy is 3× higher for home-based senior care users. Contracts per high-value customer are 2.3× higher. Good Doctor operated at a loss for 10 years to create this multiplier — this was a calculated strategic investment, not a failure.
Insight 02 · Confirmed by Good Doctor 2025 IR
The business model has been "validated"
Gross margin of 33.6% (+1.4 pps). Expense ratio decreased by 6.3 pps YoY to 30.1%. Positive operating cash flow of RMB 64M in 1H 2025. Net profit +136.8%. This is proof that unit economics are improving — it's not growth at all costs, but profitable growth.
Insight 03 · From Tech: "9+5+3" slide 33
The data flywheel creates an uncopyable moat
9 databases (5 healthcare + 3 financial + 1 operations) process 1 billion+ records per day. DeepSeek and Qwen3 are integrated. LLM calls are set to increase by 200%+ in 2025. Every interaction from 242M customers is training data. No startup could accumulate this in under 15 years.
Insight 04 · Longevity Era trend
Senior care is the "next frontier" — 250M people over 60
Good Doctor's senior care revenue grew +263.9% YoY in 1H 2025. Home-based senior care users increased +83% YoY. The government issued Guidelines on Integrated Medical and Elderly Care in 12/2024. Ping An is building 6 premium senior care communities in 5 cities — with Shanghai and Shenzhen opening in 2H 2025.
Insight 05 · Synthesized from both reports
The AI singularity is approaching — and Ping An is betting big on it
Co-CEO Michael Guo in the Strategy Update: "Tech: AI breakthrough, singularity approaching" — this is one of the 4 macrotrends Ping An's strategy revolves around. AI agent sales have already reached ~RMB 140 billion. LLM calls are set to increase by 200%+ in 2025. Ping An invested RMB 160M in R&D at Good Doctor alone in 1H 2025 — keeping it flat YoY despite improved profitability. This is a sign they are continuing to invest in the platform, not cutting costs for short-term profit optimization.
Strategic Conclusion · Barrier to Entry
Why can't this be copied in 10 years?
(1) 55,080 patents and a 30-year data infrastructure cannot be bought. (2) Relationships with 22 provincial social health insurance bureaus and the NHSA — this is political capital, not just technical capability. (3) 242M multi-product customers — the switching cost is too high when insurance + banking + family doctor are in one app. (4) Good Doctor has just been "validated" — its cost structure is improving rapidly. The barrier to entry is structural, not just competitive.
Conclusion · Looking to 2025 and Beyond
Ping An isn't building an insurance company with a healthcare add-on — they are building a "lifecycle operating system"
The 2025 Annual Results will be the time to test 3 hypotheses: (1) Will the Health & Senior Care strategy continue to contribute to NBV growth? (2) Can Good Doctor maintain its profitability trend and move towards a double-digit net margin? (3) Will AI agent sales (RMB 140 billion in 2024) accelerate to become a primary revenue source? If all three happen, Ping An will be the definitive case study of insurtech + healthtech + fintech convergence of this decade.
